## 🔥 What This App Actually Does

### 🧩 1. Understand Your Medical Report (Instantly)

Upload a report → get:

* Disease/domain detection
* Abnormal values highlighted
* Simple, human explanation

👉 No medical knowledge needed.

---

### 📊 2. Know How Serious It Is (Context Matters)

* “18% of people near you have this condition”
* Trend insights (rising / stable)

👉 You’re no longer guessing.

---

### 🏥 3. Know Where to Go (Action Layer)

* Specialized hospitals only
* Smart recommendations
* “Why this hospital?” reasoning

👉 No random Google searches.

---

### 💊 4. Stay Consistent With Treatment

* Auto medicine tracking
* Smart reminders
* Adherence monitoring

👉 Because missing meds = real consequences.

---

### 🛡️ 5. Know What You’ll Pay (Before You Panic)

* Insurance coverage breakdown
* Covered / not covered
* Estimated out-of-pocket cost

👉 Removes financial uncertainty.

---

### 💬 6. Support Your Mental State (Underrated but Critical)

* AI detects stress / anxiety
* Responds like a supportive companion
* Suggests actionable steps

👉 Because health is not just physical.
